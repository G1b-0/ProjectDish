cheese sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (Cheddar, Mozzarella, or your favorite)
  • 2 tbsp butter, softened

Instructions

  1. Preheat a skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, on the skillet. Add the cheese slices on top.
  4. Place the other slice of bread, buttered side up, on top of the cheese.
  5. Cook until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown and the cheese is starting to melt.
  6. Flip the sandwich and cook until the other side is golden brown and the cheese is fully melted.
  7. Remove from heat, let it cool for a minute, then slice and serve.
