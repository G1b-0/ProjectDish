About Us

Innes Gibson

Welcome to my culinary journey! I'm a passionate chef with 2 years of experience, and I'm here to share my love for food with you.

My Story

Born and raised in Carluke, a small town just outside of Glasgow, I've always been fascinated by the power of food to bring people together. My passion for cooking led me to pursue a career in the culinary world, where I've had the opportunity to work in various kitchens and learn from talented chefs.

As I continue my academic journey by pursuing a university degree, I decided to create this website as a platform to share my favorite recipes, culinary insights, and the joy of cooking with fellow food enthusiasts.
