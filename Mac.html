Baked Mac and Cheese Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound elbow macaroni
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 ounces grated cheddar cheese
  • 8 ounces grated Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F. Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Cook macaroni according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk in flour and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Gradually whisk in milk, dry mustard, salt, and pepper. Cook, whisking constantly, until mixture comes to a boil and thickens, about 5 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in grated cheddar cheese and Monterey Jack cheese until melted and smooth.
  6. Combine macaroni and cheese sauce in the prepared baking dish. In a small bowl, mix together breadcrumbs and melted butter. Sprinkle over the top of the macaroni mixture.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and bubbly. Serve hot.
