Chicken Fajitas

Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • Sour cream, for serving
  • Salsa, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add red and yellow bell peppers, onion, and garlic and cook until vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper and cook for another minute.
  4. Warm tortillas according to package instructions.
  5. Serve chicken mixture in tortillas and top with sour cream and salsa, if desired.
